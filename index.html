Innovation licences and teaching

Research in data mining produced several linked frameworks such as SOC, SSL, LLS, ASDM, EDSE, TPM and PME. To recognise the originality of this work, three innovation licences cover large-scale pre-processing, advanced mining methods and the combined framework group.

Innovation licences

  • IL1 – pre-processing methods for large data sets.
  • IL2 – advanced mining and model improvement methods.
  • IL3 – group licence covering SOC, SSL and LLS.

These licences sit over a family of methods that can be adapted for any sector that needs repeatable data work, from agriculture and retail to education.
