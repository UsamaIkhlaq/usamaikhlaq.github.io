About

Work across Ireland, Italy and Germany has centred on large-scale data mining, modelling and system-aware analysis. Projects include agriculture, online content and intelligent systems, alongside teaching in third-level institutes.

Research in large-scale data mining produced a linked family of methods for structuring pre-processing, temporal alignment and improvement cycles. Three innovation licences cover these methods and show that the approach is original and adaptable to domains such as agriculture, security analytics, business data and education.

Research focus & background

Interest in domain-independent analytical frameworks that support large datasets, streaming text, environmental signals, behavioural systems and satellite-derived sources. The aim is to design models that scale, remain interpretable and work under real conditions. This includes digital-twin style thinking, deployment-ready modelling pipelines and document intelligence.
